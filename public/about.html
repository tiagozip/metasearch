search.tiago.zip is a metasearch engine that scrapes results from brave search, powered by cloudflare workers. it doesn't track you and, unlike many other metasearch engines such as Searx, feels fast and looks nice.

setting it as your default engine

firefox-based browsers: right-click the url bar and select "Add 'search.tiago.zip'". then, go to about:preferences#search and under "default search engine" pick "search.tiago.zip"

chromium-based browsers go to chrome://settings/searchEngines and scroll down to "Site search". click "Add" and fill it in with:

  • name: "search.tiago.zip"
  • shortcut: "search.tiago.zip"
  • url: https://search.tiago.zip/?q=%s

then save, scroll down to the newly created entry, click the "…" and "make default"

privacy

anonymous traffic analytics

we use a custom, hardened fork of plausible to get an approximate number of active users, fully anonymously. no identifiable information about you or your requests is ever stored.

results

we source our results from brave. they don't store your queries and can't see your ip address or know who you are.

data sharing

we do not collect or share your data. meow.

keyboard shortcuts

  • j / ↓ — move highlight down a result
  • k / ↑ — move highlight up a result
  • ← — show previous image's details
  • → — show next image's details
  • / — focus the search bar
  • w — open the web results tab
  • i — open the web images tab
  • n — open the web news tab
  • esc — close details panel
